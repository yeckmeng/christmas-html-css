How Christmas is celebrated in Malaysia.

Christmas at Churches:

Visit the local churches to attend Christmas service in the morning or at night. Discover the significance of the Christmas that Christians celebrate. Different churches have their own traditions, which may include prayers that gives followers an eventful Christmas.

Food:

Christmas is the loveliest time to indulge in delectable treats. Malaysians often consume medium servings of various Christmas dishes such as turkey, chicken, or lamb, along with mashed potatoes, salads, numerous vegetables, and desserts and beverages. To please their local palate, they also include a few Malaysian foods such as curries and satay

Shopping Malls:

Every Christmas, large shopping malls like AEON, TESCO, etcetera are decked out in sparkling lights, gleaming balls and stars, and glistening streamers. Large Christmas trees are usually put in commercial malls, surrounded by decorations such as wooden houses and soldiers, snow globes, toys, and others based on their themes.

Entertainment:

Watch live Christmas shows, which are typically held in shopping centers and hotels! Carol groups, live music bands, and stage entertainers offer Christmas cheer and energy to audiences.
